\begin{abstract}
\label{sec:abstract}

We propose an application layer protocol for remote car utility control, \textsf{RCUC}. Cars are getting more and more complex and there exists a great shift in controlling cars with softwares. Softwares are designed for controlling utilities in car such as radio-controller, ac-controller, side-mirror-controller and car-internals-report etc. Smart devices are getting more and more popular and they are used as a tool in many domains with help of domain specific softwares where controlling vehicles is one of them. Our protocol enables a remote user, the client to connect to a centralized server, for controlling his one of vehicles utilities with his smart device.
